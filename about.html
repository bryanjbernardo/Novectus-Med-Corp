Our Story

Novectus Medical Corporation was incorporated in 2010 with the primary mission of introducing Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) to the mainstream of the Philippine pharmaceutical market.

For its initial offering, DreamWell, a non-melatonin, all-natural sleep enhancer, Novectus focused on importation and partnered with Pascual Laboratories, Inc. and its subsidiary, Pascual Consumer Healthcare Corporation for packaging, marketing, and distribution.

In 2018, sensing the need to manage its own course in order to realize the full potential of its launch product and others in the pipeline, Novectus ventured to take charge of marketing and distribution.

In 2019, Novectus started to expand its portfolio beyond TCM and started exploring opportunities in other breakthrough and innovative healthcare products and services.

Today, as the market has reopened completely after the pandemic, Novectus is poised to launch excellent breakthrough products with its innovative partners from Asia, North America, and Europe.

Moving forward with its solid financial strength, strong entrepreneurial spirit, and efficient organizational structure, Novectus is committed to pursue a continuing fulfillment of its vision and mission in the service of healthier families.
